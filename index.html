EXCLUSIVE Dominion Church has faced allegations of money laundering and unethical behaviour. Linda Maseko investigative journalist

"Dominion, 26 July 2024 — In a shocking turn of events, the venerable Dominion Church, led by its founding elders, the Frasers, is now at the center of a high-profile investigation. The allegations? Money laundering, financial fraud, and other unethical practices that have rocked the religious community. Detective Brenard Takawira, head of the elite Special Crimes Unit (SCU), has been relentless in his pursuit of justice. His team uncovered a web of suspicious financial transactions within the church, raising serious concerns about the integrity of its operations. The SCU, known for its expertise in tracking down white-collar criminals, has been meticulously piecing together evidence against the Frasers and their inner circle.

Here are the key points of the investigation: Money Laundering: The heart of the case lies in the alleged money laundering scheme orchestrated by the church. Large sums of money flowed through various channels, obscuring their origins and purpose. The SCU suspects that illicit funds were mixed with legitimate donations, creating a complex financial web that spans continents. Unexplained Wealth: The Frasers, once revered spiritual leaders, now face scrutiny over their extravagant lifestyles. Lavish mansions, luxury cars, and overseas bank accounts have raised eyebrows among their congregation. How did they amass such wealth while preaching humility and devotion?. Undisclosed Business Ventures: The church's financial records reveal hidden business ventures—companies, real estate holdings, and offshore accounts—that were never disclosed to their followers. These ventures allegedly served as conduits for money laundering activities. Congregational Betrayal: Congregants, who once trusted the Frasers implicitly, now feel betrayed. Some have come forward with evidence of financial coercion, claiming that their donations were misused. Others report being pressured to invest in dubious ventures under the guise of "divine blessings." The Veil of Secrecy: Unlike other organizations, religious institutions like Dominion Church enjoy a veil of financial secrecy. They are not required to report financial information to the IRS or their own members. This lack of transparency has allowed corruption to fester unchecked. The Role of Auditors: Questions arise about the auditors who failed to detect irregularities within the church's finances. Were they complicit, negligent, or simply overwhelmed by the complexity of the operation? As the investigation unfolds, the faithful are left grappling with their shattered trust. The once-hallowed halls of Dominion Church now echo with whispers of scandal and betrayal. Will justice prevail, or will the Frasers continue to hide behind their sacred robes? Stay tuned for further updates as Detective Brenard Takawira and the SCU close in on the truth. The faithful demand answers, and the world watches as this spiritual saga unfolds.
